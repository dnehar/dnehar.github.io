Overview

Systems immunology across the human lifespan

Dr. Nehar-Belaid works at the intersection of systems immunology and single-cell genomics, with a focus on immune aging, early-life immunity, autoimmune disease, and vaccine biology. He completed his postdoctoral training at The Jackson Laboratory under Dr. Jacques Banchereau, in collaboration with Dr. Virginia Pascual, and later led research programs on the tumor immune microenvironment with Dr. Roel Verhaak and on immune aging with Dr. Duygu Ucar, before establishing his independent lab at Emory University School of Medicine in September 2026.

Single-cell genomics Early-life immunity Immune aging Autoimmunity / SLE Vaccine biology Systems immunology
